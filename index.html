About Me

A warm feeling to introduce myself and a promise to take the technology to the next level. Hi, I'm Shabbir Umrali, an independent web user interface, design and development engineer based in India. I love nothing more than seeing a project go from the seed of an idea right through to launch.

I have a Bachelor's Degree in Computer Sciences and have been doing what I do since 2015 at companies. I'm passionate about exploring, gear, design, making stuff, photograph. When I'm not pushing pixels, you'll find me cooking, reading books, or watching movies.

My professional web development skills range from front and back-end development to operations. I specialize in building applications specific to the business needs of my clients. I have done work in software development, front-end/back-end web and graphic design.

I've always sought out opportunities and challenges that are meaningful to me. As a web developer, I enjoy using my obsessive attention to detail, my unequivocal love for making things, and my mission-driven work ethic to literally change the world. That's why I'm excited to make a big impact at a high growth company.
